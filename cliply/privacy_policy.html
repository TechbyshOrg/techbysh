Privacy Policy for Cliply

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

Last updated: October 15, 2025 — Developer: Techbysh

Introduction

Welcome to Cliply, a lightweight text-storage and clipboard utility developed by Techbysh. Cliply allows users to store notes locally, copy them instantly with one tap, search saved entries, edit existing notes, and delete notes using swipe gestures.

This Privacy Policy explains how Cliply handles information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

Cliply is designed to protect your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

  • No personal data: We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifying information.
  • No account needed: Cliply does not require any login or registration.
  • Local note data: Notes you create are saved only on your device and not transmitted to any server.
  • No analytics or tracking: Cliply does not use analytics tools, trackers, or third-party monitoring systems.

2. Permissions

Cliply may use the following permission:

  • Clipboard Access — Used only to copy text you select from your saved notes. Clipboard data is never collected, stored, or transmitted.

Cliply does not request access to camera, contacts, media files, location, or any other sensitive permissions.

3. Data Storage & Security

All notes and text data stored in Cliply remain on your device. We do not transfer or back up any data to external servers.

Your device manages the security of your stored notes. If you need backups across devices, please use your own system-level backup options (such as Android backup services).

4. Third-Party Services

Cliply does not integrate any third-party SDKs, advertising frameworks, analytics services, cloud services, or external APIs. There are:

  • No ads
  • No tracking
  • No third-party data sharing

5. Children

Cliply does not collect personal information from children under 13. Since no data collection occurs, the app is suitable for general audiences. If you believe a child has shared data with the app (intentionally or unintentionally), please contact us so we can assist.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in Cliply’s functionality or legal requirements. When updates occur, the Last updated date at the top of this page will be modified. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

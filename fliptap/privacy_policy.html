Privacy Policy for Fliptap

Effective Date: October 15, 2025

Last updated: October 15, 2025 — Developer: TechBySH

Introduction

Welcome to Fliptap, a simple and intuitive counter app with motion and touch controls developed by TechBySH. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App. By using Fliptap you accept the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

Fliptap is designed to minimize data collection. We do not collect personal information or identify individual users.

  • No personal data: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.
  • No account required: The App does not require registration or sign-in.
  • Local interaction data: All interactions (flips, shakes, taps) are processed locally on your device and are not transmitted to any server.

2. Permissions

To provide the core experience, Fliptap may request the following device permissions:

  • Motion sensors (accelerometer / gyroscope) — used to detect flips and shakes so the counter can increment via motion. Detection is processed on-device; no sensor data is uploaded.
  • Vibration / Haptic feedback — used to provide tactile feedback when interacting with the app.

These permissions are optional and only used when you enable the corresponding features in the App.

3. Data Storage & Security

Fliptap stores any counters and app settings locally on your device only. We do not transmit, store, or back up this data to our servers.

Because data remains on your device, please ensure you have any preferred backups or device-level protections in place if you require persistence across devices.

4. Third-Party Services

Fliptap does not integrate third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or crash reporting by default. There are:

  • No ads
  • No trackers
  • No external analytics

5. Children

Fliptap does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since the App does not collect personal data, it is suitable for general audiences. If you believe your child provided information to the App, please contact us and we will take reasonable steps to address the situation.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes in the App or legal requirements. When we make changes we will update the Last updated date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
